Partnership With Cora Spencer Elementary to Teach Recycling to 1st Graders

On Earth Day--April 22, 2020--the team at Trash Monkey along with the amazing staff at Cora Spencer Elementary School will be hosting an event that will teach the 1st Graders there about Recycling, and which items need to be recycled.
